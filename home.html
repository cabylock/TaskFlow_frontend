      Frequently Asked Questions

      What is TaskFlow?

      TaskFlow is a web application that helps you manage your tasks, track your progress, and improve your productivity.

      How do I distinguish level of task

      In the Tasks or Notifications, you can see the level of task by the color of the task:

      • Red: High priority
      • Orange: Medium priority
      • Green: Low priority

      How do I search for an event quickly?

      Look at the calendar. You will see some days marked with events. Click on a marked day to see the event details.

      • Green: Event is running
      • Orange: Event is ending
      • Red: Both of them

      How do I change my profile picture?

      To change your profile picture, click on the "Profile" button in the sidebar, then click on the "Upload Photo" button. Select a new profile picture from your computer and click on the "Save" button to update your profile picture.

      How do I change my password?

      To change your password, click on the "Profile" button in the sidebar, then enter your new password in the "New Password" field. Click on the "Save" button to update your password.

      How do I change the background color?

      To change the background color, click on the "Profile" button in the sidebar, then select a new background color from the "Background" dropdown menu. Click on the "Save" button to update the background color.
